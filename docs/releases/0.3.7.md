# ProbRAW 0.3.7

ProbRAW 0.3.7 is a visual-correction and sharpness/MTF analysis release. Its
main goal is making the ProbRAW preview match TIFF output in other
color-managed applications while keeping real-scale inspection faithful to the
digital data without interpolation.

## Main Changes

- Preview and TIFF export share the same effective recipe and ICC path, so
  chromatic adjustments visible in Photoshop or other applications are also
  visible in ProbRAW.
- The viewer preserves the current center when zooming and, at real scale or
  higher, uses exact pixel sampling without smoothing.
- A pixel grid appears at analysis magnifications for inspecting the digital
  image structure.
- Color and Contrast separates manual adjustments from ICC calibration, with
  white balance first and expanded light, color and grading controls.
- ESF/LSF/MTF plots show sample counts, pixel scale, MTF50 and MTF50P.
- Auto Sharpness uses MTF50P, halo and post-Nyquist energy to avoid selecting
  oversharpened results.
- MTF recalculation pauses when leaving Sharpness and resumes only when that tab
  is opened again.
- Chart-generated ICC profiles do not carry manual color, contrast or detail
  adjustments.

## Verification

- Full test suite: `325 passed, 1 warning`.
- External tools: ArgyllCMS `colprof`, `xicclu`, `cctiff`; ExifTool; and
  AMaZE-enabled `rawpy-demosaic` report `ok`.
- Focused tests cover preview/export equivalence, non-interpolated zoom,
  ROI/MTF persistence, Auto Sharpness, deferred MTF outside Sharpness and
  separation of chart ICC from manual adjustments.
